BUSINESS RULES: Clinic Appointment System

A patient cannot book two appointments with the same doctor at the same time.

A doctor cannot have overlapping appointments.

Only the admin can confirm or cancel appointments in version one.

A patient can request cancellation, but the appointment status should only change after admin approval.

Each doctor has working days and working hours.

The system should generate available slots based on doctor working hours and appointment duration.

A doctor can mark a day as unavailable.

If a doctor is unavailable on a specific day, patients should not see slots for that day.

Appointment confirmation notification should be sent after the admin confirms the appointment.

The system must store patient name, phone number, email, date of birth, and gender.
